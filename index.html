About

Security knowledge.
Design craft.
Human empathy.

I am a Digital Product Designer with an unusual combination: over 20 years of design leadership, a BS in Computer Science, and an MS in Cybersecurity. I design products where the stakes are real — HIPAA violations, security breaches, compliance failures.

Born in South Africa, based in Tampa, FL. I came to cybersecurity through compliance documentation work, healthcare communications at Moffitt Cancer Center, and a career-long obsession with making complex systems legible to the humans who depend on them.

I do not just make security tools look better. I understand the threats they are designed to stop.
